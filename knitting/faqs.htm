Frequently Asked Questions

What methods of payment are available?
Credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal are all available for online payment

How can I track my order status?
After you place your order on our site, you should receive an email giving you the tracking information for your order. If you put the information into our website, you'll be able to see the progress and location of your order.

How many coupons can I apply to my order?
Only one coupon can be applied per purchase, however, coupons cannot be applied to on-sale items.

If a product I'm ordering doesn't have enough in stock, how can I get my complete order?
If a product doesn't have your full order in stock, contact our customer service at 555-555-5555 to see other options for ordering.

Do you offer price matching to other stores?
At this time we're not able to price match online.
