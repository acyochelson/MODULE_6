About Us

The Knitting Corner is an online business owned by Alyssa Yochelson in North Carolina. She started it in 2020 with the goal of helping people find any of the knitting and crochet supplies they need. We believe that the only limit is your creativity and our mission is to inspire this creativity in everyone.

Policy

Privacy Policy
We want to ensure your security so you trust that your information is safe with us. We do store customer information on our website, but no information is sold to third-party sites. Some of the information we store are cookies that we use to personalize your shopping experience with us. If you aren't comfortable with this, you are able to disable cookies, but our site may not work properly.

Return Policy
We take returns up to 30 days within receiving your purchase. Contact us to get a return label to mail back your return.

Shipping Policy
We have a standard shipping option and an express shipping option. Both of these ship through USPS. For our standard shipping, we charge 7% of your cart total and you should expect your shipment to arrive withing 6-10 business days. For express shipping, we charge 25% of your cart total and you should expect your shipment within 3 business days.
