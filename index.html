London International Website

The London International Website was an assignment for my Junior year EComm class in high school to design a website to help people plan a trip for a city abroad. I chose London as my city and included information on the site such as hotels to book, transportation options, places to visit, and more. The Knitting Corner

The Knitting Corner was my capstone project for my Junior year EComm class in high school. The assignment was to make a fake E-Commerce website, and I chose to make mine for selling knitting and crochet supplies. The site contains several pages including a "Catalog" page that can be used to navigate all of the products on the site, and product-description pages with drop-down lists for selecting quantity and type.
