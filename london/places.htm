Places to Visit in London

Big Ben Big Ben is a clock tower at the Houses of Parliament. It was built in 1844 and stands at 315 feet tall.
London Eye The London Eye is a 443 foot tall ferris wheel in London. It was built in 1999 to memorialize the millennium coming to an end.
Tower Bridge Tower Bridge is a suspension bridge over River Thames. It was built between 1886 and 1894 and has two towers connected by two walkways
