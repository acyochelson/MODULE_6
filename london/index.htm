Welcome to The Big Smoke

London is one of England's most alluring cities for travelers. Their museums, royal parks, and beautiful architecture are just a few of the reasons why people love this history rich city.

Fun Facts About London

Population: 9 million.
Time Change: 5 hours ahead of EST time zone.
Climate: Cool winters, warm summers, precipitation all year round.
Language: English.
Best Time of Year to Visit: The best times of year to visit London are in the spring and the fall because of their mild weather.
