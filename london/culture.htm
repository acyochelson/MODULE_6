Cultural Differences in London

Americans are considered more outspoken and this can appear as rude to those in the UK because they are generally more reserved.

In England drinking is ingrained into social events much more than in the US

When eating out, portion sizes in the UK are smaller than in the US

London is known for its galleries and museums. In fact, 3 of the top 10 museams in the world are located in London.

While American football is the most popular sport in the US, soccer (or football in the UK) is popular in England
