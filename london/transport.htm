Transportation in London

Public Buses Buses are one of the main modes of transportation in London. They run 24-hr and offer tours throughout the city. Although these buses only take contactless payment (no cash), once you've paid you can take unlimited rides for free within the hour of payment.
Taxis In London, taxis are a great way to get around the city. Taxis are metered and have a minimum fee of £3.20 and are available for hire from the street or by telephone
The London Underground The London Underground, also called “the Tube”, is the underground railway system running underneath the city of London. The Tube usually runs from 5 a.m. to midnight. A single journey is about £2.00-£5.00 and varies by the distance traveled and the time of day.
