Local Cuisine

Full English Breakfast This dish usually consists of:
  • Sausages
  • Eggs
  • Mushrooms
  • Tomatoes
  • Blood pudding
  • Potatoes
  • Toast
Eton Mess This dessert consists of:
  • Crushed meringue
  • Cream
  • Strawberries
Sticky Toffee Pudding This dessert is made of sponge cake stuffed with raisins or dates soaked in a toffee sauce and served with custard or ice cream
