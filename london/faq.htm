London FAQs

How often does it rain? In London it rains about 100 days out of the year so it would be best to bring a raincoat or umbrella.
What should I pack to wear? For the majority of the year London stays under 70 °F so no matter when you go, you should pack layers including items like t-shirts, cardigans, and coats.
Can I visit London on a low budget? Even with a low budget, there are plenty of accommodations you can make to make your trip to London affordable. One option is to book an Airbnb instead of a hotel. Another option is to visit some of London’s free attractions. These include places like art galleries, museums, and many more.
Is London a good place to take a family with kids? London has many fun experiences for the whole family. The natural history museum, markets, and various parks are great places to go that both kids and adults will enjoy.
What is the standard for tipping? When eating out it’s standard to tip 10-15% unless it’s added onto the bill as a service fee.
